Shigeru Miyamoto

The Genius Behind the Games

Here is a time line of Mr. Miyamoto's life:

  • 1952 - Born in Sonobe, Japan
  • 1975 - Graduated from Kanzawa Municiple College of Industrial Arts with a degree in industrial design.
  • 1977 - Hired by Nintendo as an apprentice in the planning department.
  • 1979 - Helped create the art for the company's first original arcade video game, Sherrif.
  • 1981 - Designed Donkey Kong, drawing inpiration from PopEye, Beauty and the Beast and King Kong.
  • 1983 - Created Mario Bros. based on the playable character from Donkey Kong.
  • 1985 - Designed Super Mario Bros. (along with Takashi Tezuka) for the NES.
  • 1986 - Designed The Legend of Zelda (along with Takashi Tezuka) for the NES.
  • 1988 - Produced Super Mario Bros. 3 for the NES.
  • 1991 - Designed F-Zero for the SNES.
  • 1992 - Produced Super Mario Kart for the SNES.
  • 1993 - Designed and Produced Star Fox for the SNES.
  • 1998 - Produced The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time for the Nintendo64.
  • 2001 - Produced Pikmin for the GameCube.
  • 2005 - Co-produced Nintendogs for the NintendoDS.
  • 2006 - Produced The Legend of Zelda: Twighlight Princess for the Wii.
  • 2007 - Produced Super Mario Galaxy for the Wii.
  • 2009 - Produced New Super Mario Bros. Wii for the Wii.
  • 2017 - Creative Director for Super Mario Odyssey for the Switch.
